Banarasi Silk Saree - A Timeless Classic

This exquisite Banarasi silk saree is handwoven by master artisans in Varanasi, a city renowned for its silk weaving heritage dating back to the Mughal era. Each thread tells a story of tradition and craftsmanship passed down through generations.

The saree features intricate zari work using real silver and gold threads, creating beautiful traditional motifs including floral patterns, peacocks, and Mughal-inspired designs. The rich maroon base color is complemented by golden accents, making it perfect for weddings, festivals, and special occasions.

Key Features:

  • Made from pure Banarasi silk with real zari work
  • Traditional motifs inspired by Mughal architecture
  • Includes matching unstitched blouse piece
  • Comes in premium gift packaging
  • Certificate of authenticity included

The fabric has a luxurious drape and sheen that enhances your silhouette. The weight and texture are ideal for creating elegant pleats and pallu arrangements. This saree is an investment piece that will be cherished for years and passed down as a family heirloom.
